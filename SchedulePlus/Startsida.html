Schedule+

Inloggning

Schedule+ was a project started by three high school students, about to graduate. The project was labeled as "Gymnasierabete" and is a requirement to graduate. The idea came to our mind while brainstorming and bringing up spontaneous idea. The idea struck us as the correct once, it fits the main goal of the project, and involves mechanics and procedures used in earlier courses. Programming and webdevelopment are two of the certain areas. The project began in early september of 2017, with the goal of completeing the project in january of 2018. Initially, we spent time sketching the computer based program and the website you are currently visiting. A lot of hours of sweat and blood has been put into this project and we have performed at our max. It could not have been done thanks to dedicated coders on forums, and especially our teachers, who taught us to code both in C# (C-Sharp) and in HTML-5 (website). We were aspiried by the current stressful schedule each student has, and we are aware that not everyone keeps a clean schedule with everything written down. Most people rely on techonology, so why not bring together two important parts, a schedule you can manage online and on your pc.
